Phase 1: The Training Split

This program splits the body into functional movement patterns. You will train 5 days a week, with 2 dedicated rest days for growth.

Day Focus Key Lifts
Monday Upper (Power) Bench Press, Barbell Row
Tuesday Lower (Power) Squat, Romanian Deadlift
Wednesday Rest / Mobility Walking, Stretching
Thursday Push (Hypertrophy) Incline Dumbbell Press, Lateral Raise
Friday Pull (Hypertrophy) Pull-Ups, Bicep Curls
Saturday Legs (Hypertrophy) Leg Press, Lunges
Sunday Complete Rest Sleep, Eat

Phase 2: The Caloric Surplus

You cannot build a house without bricks. To grow muscle tissue, you must eat in a Caloric Surplus.

The Strategy

We do not "dirty bulk". We aim for a controlled surplus of 250-300 calories above your maintenance level. This minimizes fat gain while maximizing muscle tissue synthesis.

Sample Macro Split

For a 180lb Male in a surplus:

  • Protein: 200g (Building Blocks)
  • Carbs: 350g (Training Fuel)
  • Fats: 70g (Hormonal Health)
